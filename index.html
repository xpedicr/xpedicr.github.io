Hi! I am Rohit

I am a Research Assistant Professor at AGH UST, Krakow, Poland. I am working on the modelling of techniques that can have a direct impact on the policy by providng viable solutions for policy making. Before that, I was a Post-Doctoral Fellow at School of Mechanical Engineering Tel Aviv University, Israel. I was listed in the Indian Researchers in Stanford University’s Top 2% Most Influential Scientists in the World for 2020. I was also a member of AI-ML-NLP Group of Indian Institute of Technology Patna.

WHAT I DO

RESEARCH INTEREST

Dealing with Health-Economic Dilemma

Analysing the impact of social distancing and lockdowns during COVID-19 and providing viable solutions using Multi-objective optimization techniques.

Time Series Forecasting Modelling

Forecasting the impact of COVID-19 in the 15 Most affected countries of the world using Genetic Programming.

Nature Inspired Computing

The aim is to develop new improved algorithms. I employed different ideas and logics such as hybridization that combine the strength of different mutation strategies to add diversity in the solution space, and improve the algorithms.

Application of Nature Inspired Algorithms to Real world Problems

Optmization of Linear Antenna Arrays, Engineering Design Problems, Frame Design Problems, Wireless Sensor Networks, Image Thresholding and others.
